# Site Types and Audit Reporting

## Common Issues by Site Type

### SaaS / Product Sites
- Product pages lack content depth
- Comparison/Alternative pages missing
- Feature pages thin on content

### E-commerce
- Thin category pages
- Duplicate product descriptions
- Missing product schema

### Content / Blogs
- Outdated content not refreshed
- Keyword cannibalization
- Missing author pages

### Local Business
- Inconsistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone)
- Missing local schema
- No Google Business Profile optimization

## Audit Report Structure

1. **Executive Summary**: Overall health and top 3-5 priority issues.
2. **Technical SEO Findings**: Issue, Impact, Evidence, and Fix.
3. **On-Page SEO Findings**: Content and structural improvements.
4. **Content Findings**: Quality and E-E-A-T analysis.
5. **Prioritized Action Plan**: Critical fixes vs. Long-term goals.
