# Content Quality and E-E-A-T

## E-E-A-T Signals

### Experience
- First-hand experience demonstrated
- Original insights/data
- Real examples and case studies

### Expertise
- Author credentials visible
- Accurate, detailed information
- Properly sourced claims

### Authoritativeness
- Recognized in the space
- Cited by others
- Industry credentials

### Trustworthiness
- Accurate information
- Transparent about business
- Secure site (HTTPS)

## User Engagement Signals

- **Time on page**: Indicates content relevance
- **Bounce rate in context**: High bounce on info pages can be fine; on conversion pages is a problem
- **Pages per session**: Indicates site-wide relevance
- **Return visits**: Strong signal of authority and trust
